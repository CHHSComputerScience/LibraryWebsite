Policies

Books may be checked out for 2 weeks at a time and renewed when necessary. Please. . . . . Be very quiet. Do not eat or drink during your visit. Return books when they are due. Take care of the library.

About Library

Carbon Hill High School is full of amazing books! The range of genres go from historic books to fiction stories.There are over 5,000 books total. There is an Ipad mounted to the front desk where you can access the books in the library to find out if they are in or if they've been checked out. There are 8 computers located in the library that are accessible whenever the library is opened.There are 30 computers located in the media lab which are also accessible through the library. There are plenty of chairs throughout the library to sit and read or study.
