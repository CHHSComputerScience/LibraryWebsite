About Library

The library offers a wide range of print and digital resources for the school community. Books may be checked out for two weeks at a time and renewed when necessary. There are 8 computers located within the library and 30 computers in the media lab adjacent to the library. The furniture throughout allows ample space to sit and read or study.

Please be very quiet. Do not eat or drink during your visit. Return books when they are due. Always take care of the library.
